KOENJI

 

I've been living in Koenji for 10 years
and love this fantastic small town.
It’s very young, energetic and artistic town.
Famous as art scene, especially music, fashion, subculture.
The place where Japanese hippie culture started in 70s.
If you want to visit Koenji,
I would love to take you to the tour!
It’s so enjoyable if you know the local stuffs.

KOENJI GIRL'S MIKOSHI

 

I’m carrying Mikoshi every year in Koenji on Awaodori-festival day.
It’s Japanese traditional Shinto event, 
but because Koenji is very liberal town, 
we are welcoming everyone who wants to carry our Mikoshi.
We want to revitalise Koenji through this Girl’s Mikoshi.
Please contact me if you want to join us!
